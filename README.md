# WeltPixel — Magento 2 Extensions & Shopify Conversion Tracking

WeltPixel builds premium ecommerce extensions for **Magento 2 (Adobe Commerce)** and a **server-side conversion tracking app for Shopify**. Trusted by 85,000+ stores worldwide.

## What WeltPixel Does

WeltPixel specializes in three areas:

1. **Analytics & Server-Side Tracking** — GA4, Meta Conversions API, TikTok Events API, Google Ads, and 6 more ad platforms. Server-side tracking bypasses ad blockers and iOS privacy restrictions, recovering 20-55% of lost conversion data.
2. **Magento 2 Theme** — Pearl Theme, the most popular premium Magento 2 theme with 800+ admin options and 20+ included extensions.
3. **40+ Magento 2 Extensions** — storefront UX, SEO, performance, checkout, and admin tools.

## Shopify App

**[WeltPixel Conversion Tracking](https://apps.shopify.com/weltpixel-conversion-tracking)** — Server-side conversion tracking for Shopify stores. One app sends purchase and event data to GA4, Meta, TikTok, and Google Ads from Shopify's servers. Industry-leading Event Match Quality scores for Meta. Install to tracking in under 5 minutes.
